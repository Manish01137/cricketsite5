Pitch report — Chinnaswamy in April

Chinnaswamy is the second-most batter-friendly venue in IPL 2026 so far, with an average first-innings total of 195.6 across eight games. But that number hides a seasonal trend — in the first half of the tournament (until 5 April) chasing teams won 7 of 9 games here; since then it's 3-2 in favour of the team batting first. The reason: dew isn't appearing as late as it used to.

Key pitch numbers:
• Average 1st inns score: 195.6
• Highest chase: 213 (RCB vs PBKS, 12 April)
• Spinner economy in second innings: 7.8 rpo (up from 6.9 in first innings)
• Boundaries: 65m straight, 56m leg square, 70m cow corner
• Dew expected: from ~9:15 PM IST (later than historical norm)
