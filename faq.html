Markets & betting

Which leagues do you cover?

IPL, WPL, T20I, ODI, Test cricket, BBL, PSL, CPL, The Hundred, ILT20, SA20, LPL, Ranji Trophy, SMAT, Vijay Hazare, County Championship, T20 Blast, U-19 internationals — basically any cricket on Earth with a TV feed.

What's the difference between Exchange and Sportsbook?

Exchange = peer-to-peer betting where you can back or lay any outcome at user-set odds (sharper prices). Sportsbook = fixed odds set by the bookmaker (simpler, slower-moving prices). You can use either or both — most users start with Sportsbook and graduate to Exchange.

What is "fancy" / "session" / "lambi"?

Fancy = micro-markets within a match (boundary in next over, runs in next over). Session = total runs scored in a defined block of overs (e.g. 6-over total, 15-over total). Lambi = total individual batsman score. All three are extensively offered.

What's the maximum payout on a single bet?

₹50,00,000 (50 lakhs) per bet. If you want to stake larger, contact your account manager and we'll arrange a one-off ceiling lift.

Can I cash out mid-match?

Yes — every market on Exchange supports partial or full cash-out at the prevailing price. Sportsbook offers cash-out on most main markets but not on Fancy/Session.

Security, legal & responsible gaming

Is online cricket betting legal in India?

Online betting laws vary state by state. Some states (Sikkim, Goa, Daman) explicitly permit online gaming. Others rely on the 1867 Public Gambling Act, which courts have interpreted differently for "skill" vs "chance" games. CricVault is licensed offshore (Curaçao eGaming, Ref. #8048/JAZ) and you are responsible for understanding the rules in your state. The service is not available in states that explicitly prohibit online gaming.

Is my money safe?

Yes. User balances are held in a segregated client-funds account, separate from operating funds. We're audited annually under our Curaçao license and use bank-grade 256-bit encryption end-to-end.

Is my data shared with third parties?

Never sold. Shared only with: (1) our payment processor for transaction settlement, (2) our identity-verification partner during KYC, (3) law enforcement when legally compelled. Read the full Privacy Policy.

Can I close my account?
